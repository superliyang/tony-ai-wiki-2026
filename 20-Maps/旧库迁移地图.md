## Migration Rhythm

1. Use imported areas as source of truth until a topic is promoted.
2. Promote only bounded slices: one domain, one map, one project, or one playbook at a time.
3. Keep backlinks from the new note to the imported source.
4. Update this map when a whole area graduates into a current formal domain.
