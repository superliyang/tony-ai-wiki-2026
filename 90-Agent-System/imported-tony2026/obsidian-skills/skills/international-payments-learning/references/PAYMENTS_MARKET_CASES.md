# Payments Market Cases

## Purpose

Use market cases to translate general payment theory into region-specific judgment.

## Suggested Regions

- North America
- Europe
- Southeast Asia
- Latin America
- India
- Japan and Korea
- Middle East and Africa

## Suggested Priority Countries

- United States
- United Kingdom
- Brazil
- India
- Japan

## What Each Market Case Should Cover

1. Market profile
2. Dominant payment methods and user preference
3. Main success-rate drivers
4. Risk and chargeback characteristics
5. Channel and operating advice
6. One or two business cases

## What Each Country Deep Dive Should Cover

1. Why the country deserves separate focus
2. Local payment-method structure
3. Main conversion and authorization considerations
4. Main risk, dispute, or compliance issues
5. Business-model fit
6. Entry checklist
7. Related regional case and playbooks
