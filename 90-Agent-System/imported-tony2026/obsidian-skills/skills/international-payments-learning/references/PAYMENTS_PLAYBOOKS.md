# Payments Playbooks

## Purpose

Use playbooks when the learner already knows the basic payments framework, but now needs to apply it to a concrete business model.

## Suggested Playbooks

- SaaS 订阅支付作战手册
- 跨境电商支付作战手册
- Marketplace 平台支付作战手册
- B2B 收款与对公支付作战手册
- 跨境汇款与代发作战手册

## What Each Playbook Should Cover

1. Why this business model is hard
2. North-star metrics for the scenario
3. Core payment design choices
4. Risk, chargeback, or compliance priorities
5. Technical and operating focus
6. Business cases
7. Common mistakes
8. Related topics, markets, and templates
