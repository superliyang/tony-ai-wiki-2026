# LiveOps 运营节奏手册

## 目标

把一款 skills game 从“可玩”推进到“值得每天回来”。

## 最小节奏

- daily challenge
- weekly special event
- monthly championship or ladder reset

## 团队每周最该看什么

- repeat participation
- qualifier completion
- event participation
- dispute volume
- payout/support issues
