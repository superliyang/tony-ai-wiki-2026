# Hackathon 范围裁剪模板

## 必做

- one playable loop
- one scoring model
- one results screen
- one pitch story

## 可做

- one progression hook
- one event or daily mission
- one social or replay hook

## 不做

- large platform abstractions
- complex economy trees
- too many game modes
- too many asset styles
