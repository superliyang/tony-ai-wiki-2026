## Current Operating Model

```text
Capture -> Recall -> Judge -> Crystallize -> Reuse
```

- Capture: ChatGPT Desktop, Hermes, Weixin, Feishu, OpenHuman, manual notes.
- Recall: Hermes, Obsidian search, agent-memory, hot cache.
- Judge: Tony decides what deserves attention.
- Crystallize: Codex turns accepted material into durable notes, maps, playbooks, or skills.
- Reuse: Obsidian, GitHub, Codex, Hermes, ChatGPT, and future agents reuse the assets.
